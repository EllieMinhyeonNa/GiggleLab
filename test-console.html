All results show in the console. Press Cmd+Option+J (Mac) or F12 (Windows) to open it.

Quick Start:

1. Open the console

2. Enter your API key once:

API_KEY = "your-gemini-api-key-here"

3. Test any message + tone:

test("I got the job!", "excited")

Available tones:

excited, laughing, loving, crying, nervous, pleading, surprised

Examples:

test("This is amazing!", "laughing")
test("You mean everything to me", "loving")
test("I can't believe this", "crying")

Test all tones at once:

testAll("I got the job!")
